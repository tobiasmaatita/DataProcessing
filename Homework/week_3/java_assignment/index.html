For this assignment, I plotted a line graph containing data from the NBA Finals between 1980 and 2017. I incorporated two types of information: first, the mean number of points scored in the Finals by both the champions and the runners up. Second, the percentage of threepointers scored by both the champions and the runners up. I hoped to show that scoring threepointers has become a more important aspect of basketball over the years. Indeed, a small increase of the percentage of threepointers can be detected while the average number of scored points stays level. However, from this data one should not infer that threepointers are more common today.

Navigate via the buttons in the navigation bar. To see the average number of points scored by the champions and runners up, choose 'Points'. 'Threes' shows the percentage of scored threepointers. 'Both' renders a linegraph of both the points and threepointers, to compare the different trends.

A link to the data used for these graphs can be found under the 'Data source' button.
