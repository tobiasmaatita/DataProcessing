For this assignment, I plotted a line graph containing data from the NBA Finals between 1980 and 2017. I incorporated two types of information: first, the mean number of points scored in the Finals by both the champions and the runners up. Second, the percentage of threepointers scored by both the champions and the runners up. I hoped to show that scoring threepointers has become a more important aspect of basketball over the years.
